History

Cross River State Assets Management and Recovery was established to protect, preserve, and judiciously manage the state's valuable assets for the benefit of its citizens. The need for effective assets management became clear as the government sought to identify, account for, and recover properties and resources that had been mismanaged or left dormant over the years. The initiative began with an extensive assessment of the state’s assets, revealing opportunities for optimized resource use and long-term economic benefits for the state.

Through dedicated efforts, Cross River State Assets Management and Recovery has transformed how assets are tracked, recovered, and maintained. This transformation is critical for ensuring that the resources of Cross River State are used efficiently, transparently, and sustainably. By instating clear guidelines for asset recovery and promoting accountability, this agency stands as a symbol of responsible stewardship, working to restore and maximize the state's resources.

Mission Vision Integrity

Mission Statement

The mission of Cross River State Assets Management and Recovery is to safeguard and optimize the state’s assets through strategic recovery, meticulous management, and sustainable utilization. We are committed to enhancing the value of government properties and resources, ensuring that they contribute to the economic and social growth of the state. Our goal is to foster an environment where state assets are accounted for, transparently managed, and protected from misuse.

We are focused on fostering efficient asset management practices that yield measurable returns for the state and improve the lives of our citizens. Every effort we undertake is dedicated to building a legacy of sustainable asset stewardship, where our resources are harnessed effectively for the benefit of all.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to become a model institution for state assets management and recovery, recognized for its commitment to transparency, accountability, and excellence. We aspire to establish a robust framework that sets a standard for asset management, recovery, and preservation, driving a sustainable future for Cross River State.

Through proactive policies and unwavering dedication, we envision a Cross River State where every asset, whether physical or financial, serves a purpose and contributes to the welfare of the public. Our vision is to build a strong foundation of resources that will empower future generations, ensuring that our state's assets are a source of pride and prosperity for all.

Integrity Statement

Integrity lies at the heart of Cross River State Assets Management and Recovery. We uphold the highest standards of honesty, accountability, and ethical conduct in all our operations. Recognizing the importance of trust in public service, we are dedicated to transparent practices and diligent stewardship of the state's resources.

Our commitment to integrity is reflected in our rigorous asset tracking, recovery procedures, and transparent financial reporting. By fostering a culture of openness and respect for the rule of law, we strive to inspire public confidence in our work. Every asset recovered, every transaction made, and every record kept is a testament to our dedication to serving Cross River State with integrity. We are devoted to ensuring that all assets are handled responsibly, equitably, and in a manner that upholds the principles of public service.
